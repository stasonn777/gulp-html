About Us

We are a marketing agency specialising in mobile app promotion. Our solutions cover all key aspects of mobile marketing, from in-app shop optimisation to large-scale advertising campaigns and collaboration with opinion leaders. We help app developers increase visibility, engage their target audience and scale growth through an integrated approach.

Our Marketing Services

App Store Optimisation (ASO):
We improve app positions in the App Store and Google Play by optimising titles, keywords, descriptions, icons and screenshots. Regular listing audits, monitoring rankings and conversion rates help us to stay relevant and increase organic installs.
Generate organic traffic:
Create content marketing and social media presence strategies that drive interest and engagement. Publish valuable content, initiate discussions, engage with your audience and build a loyal community around your product.
Paid Advertising (Performance Marketing):
We develop and launch effective campaigns on Google Ads, Meta (Facebook, Instagram) and other advertising networks. Precise targeting, CPI optimisation and ROAS analysis allow you to attract relevant audiences and control the ROI of your budget.
Marketing through Influencers:
Select and establish cooperation with bloggers, streamers and content-makers whose audience matches the profile of your app. Organise reviews, demos and native integrations that build trust and interest in the product.
